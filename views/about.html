About Little Heaven Daycare

We nurture children’s natural curiosity through play-based learning experiences. Our approach focuses on building strong emotional, cognitive, and social foundations, helping children develop confidence, empathy, and independence.Our caring and experienced staff create a warm, inclusive atmosphere where every child feels safe, supported, and inspired to explore the world around them.

"A nurturing, safe, and fun environment where your child can grow and shine"

🎯 Our Mission

To support early childhood development through play, creativity, and compassionate care. We believe that children grow best in an environment that values their uniqueness and fosters confidence, independence, and a lifelong love of learning.

📚 Our Approach

  • 📖 Language and literacy development
  • 🎨 Creative arts and imaginative play
  • 🔢 Early math and science skills
  • 🏃‍♂️ Gross and fine motor development
  • 💬 Social-emotional learning and problem-solving
