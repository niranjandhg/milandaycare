WELCOME!

At Little Heaven Home Daycare, we provide a nurturing and inclusive environment where children grow, learn, and thrive. Our licensed programs support infants, toddlers, preschoolers, and school-aged children with activities designed to spark curiosity, creativity, and confidence. Conveniently located in the Eastern Side of Regina, Saskatchewan, Little Heaven is licensed by Early Learning and Child Care through the Saskatchewan Ministry of Education. Our caring educators follow a child-centered approach, adapting to each child’s unique interests and developmental needs.

Why Choose Us?

  • Safe & nurturing environment
  • Licensed by Early Learning and Child Care, SK
  • Qualified, passionate educator
  • Daily activities that promote creativity, literacy, and life skills
  • Nutritious snacks and meals provided
  • Conveniently located in Regina with easy access
